Ellen DeGeneres



Ellen Lee DeGeneres is an American comedian, television host, actress, writer, and producer. She starred in the sitcom Ellen from 1994 to 1998 and has hosted her syndicated TV talk show, The Ellen DeGeneres Show, since 2003.

Born: January 26, 1958 (age 62 years), Metairie, Louisiana, United States
Education: University of New Orleans
Spouse: Portia de Rossi (m. 2008)
Parent(s):Betty DeGeneres (mother) & Elliot DeGeneres (father)
Best Known For:Stand-Up Comedy & Television Talk Show
