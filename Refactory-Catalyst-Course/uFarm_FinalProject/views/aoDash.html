Welcome to the Ufarm Dashboard !


As the Agricultural Officer, you have access to the following functions:

  1. 1) Register a Farmer One Officer (F.O) onto this platform
  2. 2) List all the F.O on the platform & view which ward they are assigned to
  3. 3) Appoint a new F.O within an Urban Ward
  4. 4) Create or Edit Login Credentials for F.O to access this platform

To get started, click on 'Admin' in the navigation menu above
